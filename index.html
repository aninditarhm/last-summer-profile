WHO WE ARE?

Born from the desire to offer healthy and aesthetic juice options amidst the growing healthy lifestyle trend.

Inspired by the lack of unique juice bars, Last Summer provides a comfortable escape for post-workout recovery and a new juice experience. We offer fresh, healthy juices you can create, in a refreshing tropical atmosphere.
